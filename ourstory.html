Welcome to Towers Tap House!

Towers Tap House is an Austin inspired beer garden and casual restaurant located on Eldorado Parkway in Little Elm. With a laid back vibe, the Taphouse overlooks the lakes at Hydrous Wakeboard Park and Lake Lewisville with the best outdoor patio in all of North Texas. At the Taphouse, it's always "come as you are" with cold beer, great food and a one of a kind lakeside vibe.


Towers serves up scrumptious burgers, chicken, sandwiches, fish taco's, fresh salads and appetizers in a casual relaxed setting. The Taphouse boasts 16 beers on tap including a wide variety of local craft Texas beers. All of our burgers are hand prepared daily, 1/2lb top quality beef so that they are juicy and flavorful. With Happy Hours, daily chalkboard specials, outdoor bars and live music, you will love the laid back vibe at the Taphouse.


We have food, fun and games to entertain parties of all ages so come on down, whether it is to watch a game on our many TV's or to have a private party on the coolest and largest outdoor patio in North Texas!


The Hydrous cable park surrounds the Towers Tap House patio making it the perfect place for parents and spectators to sit back and enjoy all the action in a very laidback environment! Towers Tap House is known for its great food, extensive selection of draft beers and amazing views of the cablepark.
